About Computers' Companies

  • About ASUS

    ASUS is a prominent multinational computer hardware and consumer electronics company founded in 1989 in Taipei, Taiwan. The company was established by four engineers—T.H. Tung, Ted Hsu, Wayne Hsieh, and M.T. Liao—who previously worked at Acer. They named their company "ASUS," derived from the mythical creature Pegasus, symbolizing the company's commitment to innovation and excellence.

  • About Acer

    Acer Inc. is a Taiwanese multinational hardware and electronics corporation that has established itself as a key player in the global information and communications technology (ICT) industry. Founded in 1976 by Shih Chen Jung and initially named Multitech International, the company transitioned to the Acer brand in 1987. Over the decades, Acer has evolved from a small distribution company into one of the world's largest PC vendors, recognized for its innovative products and commitment to quality.

  • About Apple

    Apple Inc. is a leading American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Founded on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple has become a household name, renowned for its innovative products and services that have transformed the technology landscape.

  • About DELL

    Dell Inc. is a prominent American technology company that specializes in developing, selling, repairing, and supporting computers and related products and services. Founded in 1984 by Michael Dell while he was a student at the University of Texas, the company initially operated under the name "PC's Limited." The vision was to sell personal computers directly to customers, allowing for better customization and understanding of consumer needs.

  • About Lenovo

    Lenovo is a leading global technology company that specializes in designing, manufacturing, and selling a wide range of products, including personal computers, tablets, smartphones, and data center solutions. Founded in 1984 by Liu Chuanzhi and a group of engineers in Beijing, China, Lenovo has grown from a small startup into one of the largest PC manufacturers in the world.

  • About Toshiba

    Toshiba Corporation is a Japanese multinational conglomerate with a diverse portfolio that includes a wide range of products and services, such as power systems, industrial and social infrastructure, electronic components, and consumer electronics. Founded in 1939 through the merger of two companies—Shibaura Seisaku-sho, established in 1875, and Tokyo Denki, founded in 1890—Toshiba has a rich history that reflects Japan's technological advancements.

  • About HP

    HP Inc., commonly known as HP, is an American multinational information technology company headquartered in Palo Alto, California. Founded in 1939 by Bill Hewlett and Dave Packard, HP has played a pivotal role in the development of personal computing and printing technologies. The company originated in a small garage, which has since become a symbol of Silicon Valley's entrepreneurial spirit.

  • About IBM

    IBM, or International Business Machines Corporation, is a multinational technology company headquartered in Armonk, New York. Founded in 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company (CTR), IBM has evolved into one of the largest and most influential IT companies globally, with operations in over 170 countries.
